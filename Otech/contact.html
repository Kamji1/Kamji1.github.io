Contact

OTech, a tech blog, started in March 2020 with the website otechblog.com and contact@otechblog.com as its primary email. Over the years, it expanded its online presence with social media accounts on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn, while also adding features like a contact form, newsletter, YouTube channel, and podcast. In 2022, it established a business address in Silicon Valley and further diversified its social media platforms with Twitter @OTechBlogOfficial and TikTok @otech.trend. By 2023, OTech had updated its website, introduced new email addresses for advertising and support (ads@otechmedia.com and support@otechmedia.com), and launched a dedicated tech forum at forum.otechblog.com.
